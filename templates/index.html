I'm Zehra Mina Sarıoğlan, a graphic designer based in Ankara and a recent graduate of Bilkent University. I work across visual communication, branding, editorial design, motion graphics, and UI/UX. My approach is shaped by curiosity, storytelling, and a strong interest in how people experience design. I like turning ideas into clear, expressive, and meaningful visuals from printed pieces to digital interfaces and motion based projects.
